About Marve Beach:

  • Marve Beach is located in the Western Suburb of Malad in the city of Mumbai, India.
  • Ferry Services to the Essel World and Water kingdom Amusement Parks and Manori are available at its north end.
  • Marve beach may be reached from Malad Railway Station (West side) by BEST bus no 272. Autorickshaws and taxis are available round the clock.
  • The beach is not safe for swimming due to presence of very swift currents and sinking sand.

Nearby Attractions:
  • Essel World
  • Gorai Beach: The Gorai Beach is popular for its beautiful surroundings and villas. Ferries available at the Marve Beach are the best way to reach the Gorai Beach.

Activities To Do:
  • One will love to explore the sheer scenic beauty of these beaches in the absence of the chaotic crowd of vendors that is not so common to Mumbai beaches.
  • The beaches dotted with some of the finely managed exotic private cottages built on the backdrop of the vast Arabian Sea.
  • These cottages offer the utmost in hospitality and luxury and are definitely tempting for most that see them.

Best Time To Visit:

  • The months from October to March offer the best time at the beach when Mumbai observes less humidity.
  • Marve Beach can be visited anytime of the year to take a stroll along the beach and feel the cool breeze.
