About Juhu:

  • This beach is known to be the one of the most-visited beaches of India and is situated in 'Ville Parle'.
  • Juhu Beach is also famous for its local delicacies and street food.
  • Watching the sunset in this wonderful place, guarded by the vast Arabian Sea, is a dream-like vision for anyone who visits the beach.

Nearby Attractions:
  • Versova

Activities To Do:
  • The street foods, such as 'Pani Puri', 'Bhel Puri' and 'Pav Bhaji' are just some of the many delicacies offered at the beach stalls.
  • One can also visit the famous Italian restaurants here such as Little Italy, Mangi Ferra, Don Giovanni, Penne, and so on.
  • Exciting activities like horse and camel rides are also available at Juhu Beach.

Best Time To Visit:

  • The best time to visit Juhu Beach is from the months of October to February.
  • It is best to avoid visiting the beach during the monsoon season as high tides are expected during this time.

By Rail:
  • As Mumbai is well-connected by rail routes, reaching Juhu Beach will never be a problem.
  • Ville Parle station is the closest railway station to Juhu Beach at a distance of 3 km from the main spot.

By Air:
  • Juhu Beach is situated very close to the domestic airport, as well as the international airport.
  • One can hire a taxi or auto from the airport to the beach.
